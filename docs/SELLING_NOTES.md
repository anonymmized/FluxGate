# Selling Notes

FluxGate should be positioned as an enterprise-controlled AI traffic gateway, not as a generic proxy.

## Ideal First Customers

- Teams already spending meaningful money on OpenAI-compatible APIs.
- Enterprises that need local PII controls before prompts leave their network.
- AI product teams with repeated prompt templates and cacheable request patterns.

## Initial Paid Offering

- Private deployment assistance.
- OpenAI-compatible traffic support first.
- Local PII redaction and prompt-history trimming.
- Transparent reporting on cache hit rate, filtered tokens, p95 latency, and estimated savings.

## Claims To Avoid Until Measured

- Do not claim 40-70% savings for all customers before benchmarking their traffic.
- Do not claim zero latency overhead.
- Do not claim complete privacy unless no upstream request is made.
- Do not claim HTTP/2 support until implemented and tested.
